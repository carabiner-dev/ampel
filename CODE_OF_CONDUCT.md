# Code of Conduct

Contributors to the the AMPEL project and all other repositories under the
Policy Labs organization must adhere to the Open Source Security Foundation
[Code of Conduct](https://openssf.org/community/code-of-conduct/).

As stipulated in the Policy Labs governance charter, violations of the Code of
Conduct will be reviewed and addressed by the Steering Committee, which reserves
the right to remove contributors or maintainers who violate these terms.
